Privacy Policy

Last updated: 21 March 2026 · Version 1.0

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Workflow Platform (“we”, “us”, “our”) collects, uses, stores, and protects personal data when you use our CRM Calendar application (“the Service”). We are committed to protecting your privacy in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the Irish Data Protection Act 2018.

2. Data Controller

The data controller for the platform is the organisation that operates this instance of the Workflow Platform. If you are an employee user, your employer (the business owner) is the data controller. For questions about how your data is processed, please contact your organisation administrator.

3. Personal Data We Collect

3.1 User Account Data

  • Email address (for authentication and communications)
  • First and last name
  • Phone number (optional)
  • Role within the organisation
  • Password (stored as a one-way bcrypt hash — we never store plaintext passwords)

3.2 Employee Data

  • Department and position
  • Hire date
  • Hourly rate
  • Skills and notes

3.3 Customer Data

Business owners store customer contact records within the Service. This data is entered and managed by the business owner (data controller) and may include:

  • Customer name, email, phone number
  • Address and Eircode
  • Company name
  • Service notes
  • Notification preferences (WhatsApp, email)

3.4 Technical Data

  • IP address (logged in audit trail for security)
  • Device information from authentication tokens
  • Geolocation coordinates (derived from entered addresses via Google Maps)

4. Legal Basis for Processing

Processing Activity Legal Basis (GDPR Art. 6)
User authenticationLegitimate interest (security)
CRM operations (jobs, customers)Contractual necessity
WhatsApp / email notificationsConsent (opt-in per customer)
Audit loggingLegitimate interest (security & compliance)
Address geocodingLegitimate interest (service delivery)

5. Third-Party Data Processors

  • Twilio — WhatsApp message delivery (phone number, message content)
  • Google Maps Platform — Address geocoding (address, Eircode)
  • SMTP Provider — Email delivery (email address, message content)
  • AWS (RDS, ElastiCache) — Database and cache hosting (all stored data)

All third-party processors are bound by data processing agreements and process data only on our instructions.

6. Data Retention

  • User accounts: Retained while active. Anonymized on request (72-hour grace period).
  • Customer records: Retained while active. Anonymized on owner/admin request (immediate).
  • Notification logs: Automatically deleted after 90 days for delivered/failed entries.
  • Audit logs: Retained for up to 2 years for compliance, then deleted.
  • Authentication tokens: Access tokens expire after 30 minutes; refresh tokens after 7 days.

7. Your Rights Under GDPR

As a data subject, you have the following rights:

  • Right of Access (Art. 15) — Download all your personal data from your Profile page.
  • Right to Rectification (Art. 16) — Update your profile and contact information at any time.
  • Right to Erasure (Art. 17) — Request anonymization of your account from your Profile page.
  • Right to Data Portability (Art. 20) — Export your data in machine-readable JSON format.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent — Notification preferences can be updated at any time.

For customer data subjects: If you are a customer of a business using this platform and wish to exercise your GDPR rights, please contact the business directly. The business owner can export and anonymize your data through the application.

8. Cookies & Local Storage

This application uses only essential cookies required for authentication:

  • wp_access_token — HttpOnly, Secure, SameSite=Lax. Short-lived JWT for API authentication.
  • wp_refresh_token — HttpOnly, Secure, SameSite=Lax. Used to renew expired access tokens.

We do not use any analytics, advertising, or tracking cookies. Local Storage is used only for UI preferences (e.g., cookie consent acknowledgment, calendar view settings).

9. Security Measures

  • Passwords hashed with bcrypt (industry-standard one-way hashing)
  • Notification credentials encrypted at rest with Fernet symmetric encryption
  • HTTPS/TLS encryption for all data in transit
  • HttpOnly, Secure, SameSite cookies to prevent XSS and CSRF
  • Database encryption at rest (AWS RDS)
  • Role-based access control with fine-grained permissions
  • Comprehensive audit logging of administrative actions
  • Multi-tenant isolation — each business's data is fully separated

10. Contact & Complaints

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to exercise your data protection rights, please contact your organisation administrator.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Irish Data Protection Commission:

  • 21 Fitzwilliam Square South, Dublin 2, D02 RD28
  • Website: www.dataprotection.ie
